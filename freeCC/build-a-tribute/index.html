Albert Einstein

  • Albert Einstein was born on 14 March 1879 in Germany.
  • At the age of 16, he took the acceptance test of the Swiss Federal Polytechnic, in Zurich.
  • During his first marriage, Albert had two sons: Hans and Eduard
  • He worked at the Federal Office for Intellectual Property
  • Together wit some friends, Einstein started a discussion group called The Olympia Academy, where members discussed science and philosophy
  • He obtained a PHD from the University of Zurich
  • Became a US citizen in 1940
  • He is well know for his scientific career
  • Perhaps his most succesful scientific accomplishment was the General Theory of Relativity
